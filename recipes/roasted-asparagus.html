Roasted Asparagus

Description

Tender roasted asparagus finished with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice? Now that's what we call the perfect side of spring greens!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus, woody ends trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • lemon, juiced

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Snap off (or cut off) and discard the woody ends of the asparagus.
  2. On the baking sheet, toss the asparagus spears with olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper. Then arrange them in a flat, even layer.
  3. Bake for 7 to 12 minutes (less time for thinner spears, more time for thicker spears), until the spears are tender.
  4. Transfer the roasted asparagus to a serving platter and squeeze fresh lemon juice on top.
